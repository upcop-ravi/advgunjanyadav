Statutory Foundation

The Legal Backbone

???? ????? - ????? ?????

Behind every compliance notice lies a specific provision of the FSS Act, 2006. Understanding these pillars is critical for a robust technical defense.

Section 31

Licensing & Registration

The absolute mandate of the law: "No person shall commence or carry on any food business except under a license." This section defines the regulatory entry point for every FBO in India.

  • Registration (Turnover < ?12L)
  • State/Central License

Section 26

FBO Responsibilities

Defines the liability of manufacturers and retailers. Provides a "Due Diligence" defense for sellers if the product was sealed and tamper-proof.

Seller ShieldStrict Liability

Section 32

Improvement Notices

If an FBO fails to comply with regulations, the Designated Officer issues an Improvement Notice. Failure to comply leads to license suspension.

  • Compliance Period
  • Suspension Risk

Section 47

Sampling & Analysis

Every FBO has the right to have one part of the sample analyzed at their own cost. This is the foundation of a technical defense against lab reports.

Counter-Sample RightNABL Lab

Section 50-67

Offences & Penalties

Distinguishes between Civil Offenses (Fines up to ?10L) and Criminal Offenses (Imprisonment).

  • Sec 51: Substandard Food
  • Sec 52: Misbranded food

Section 59: Unsafe Food

"Any person who manufactures for sale or stores or sells or distributes any article of food for human consumption which is unsafe."

Punishment ranges from ?1 Lakh fine to Life Imprisonment depending on gravity.

The ADM Hearing

Adjudication Proceedings

???????? ??????? ????????? - ADM ??????

Legal Process Under Section 68 of FSS Act

Most violations are decided by the Adjudicating Officer (Additional District Magistrate level) through a formal summary process.

1

Notice Issuance

Technical scrutiny starts with a formal notice issued by the ADM/AO detailing the alleged violations.

2

Reply Filing

Drafting a "damdar" technical response within the stipulated time to counter the department's claims.

3

The Hearing

Presentation of oral arguments and documentary evidence before the Judge (Adjudicating Officer).

4

The Order

Final disposal of the case with an order of penalty or specific corrective directions for the FBO.

Note: Under Section 68, the Adjudicating Officer has the power of a Civil Court. Timely legal representation is critical to prevent heavy financial losses and license cancellation.

Food Safety FAQs

??????? ?????? ?????? - ?????? ?????
Is FSSAI licence mandatory for every food business?
Any person involved in manufacture, storage, distribution or sale of food is generally required to obtain FSSAI registration or licence depending on scale of business. Procedural guidance may be sought from a legal practitioner such as Advocate Gunjan regarding applicable category.
What is the difference between FSSAI Registration and Licence?
Small food businesses fall under basic registration, while medium and large operators require State or Central licence as per turnover and operational area.
What happens if food is sold without FSSAI licence?
Authorities may initiate proceedings which can include penalty, closure direction or prosecution depending on facts of the case and statutory provisions.
Can authorities seal a restaurant or food shop?
In certain situations involving safety or serious violation, officials may take action including sealing or seizure subject to legal procedure.
What is an Improvement Notice under Food Safety law?
It is a direction issued by the authority requiring the food business operator to rectify deficiencies within a specified time period.
Can a laboratory food sample report be challenged?
The law provides procedure for contesting or seeking re-analysis of food samples through the prescribed mechanism. Documentation and statutory timelines are important in such matters.
Where are FSSAI penalty cases heard?
Many matters are decided by the Adjudicating Officer, with further remedies available before appellate forum as per law.
Is labelling mistake also punishable?
Incorrect or misleading labelling may attract penalty depending on nature of violation under statutory regulations.
Can online food sellers or cloud kitchens require licence?
Food sold through online platforms or home kitchen operations may also require registration or licence based on scale of activity.
What should be done after receiving food safety notice?
A reply is generally required within prescribed time along with supporting documents as per legal provisions. Procedural assistance may be taken from a legal professional including Advocate Gunjan.
