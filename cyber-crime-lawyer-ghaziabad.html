Under Indian law, electronic evidence is inadmissible without a mandatory certificate (Sec 65B old Evidence Act / Sec 63 BSA). We examine these certificates for technical flaws�missing timestamps, unsigned declarations, or improper device identification. If the certificate is defective or absent, the prosecution's entire digital case can be dismissed at the trial stage.
