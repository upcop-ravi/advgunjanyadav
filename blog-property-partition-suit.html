Specialized Practice

Civil Litigation &
Property Disputes.

Qualified resolution for complex land disputes, title suits, and ancestral property partitions under Hindu Succession Act and UP Revenue Code.

Property disputes within families are common in Ghaziabad. A Partition Suit is a specific legal action to force the division of joint family property.

Steps to File

1. Legal Notice: Always start by sending a legal notice claiming your share.
2. Filing Suit: If the notice is ignored, we file a suit in the Civil Court.
3. Preliminary Decree: The court defines shares.
4. Final Decree: The property is physically divided by a commissioner.

Legal Conclusion

Every case is unique. While this guide provides a general overview of Filing a Partition Suit for Ancestral Property, proceeding without specific legal counsel can be risky.
